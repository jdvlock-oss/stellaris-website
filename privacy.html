Privacy Policy

Effective July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stellaris AI (“Stellaris,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information submitted through this website.

When you submit the contact form, we may collect your name, work email address, company, and the information included in your message. We may also receive basic website usage information such as page views, referring pages, browser type, device type, and approximate location through privacy-conscious web analytics.

How we use information

We use submitted information to respond to inquiries, evaluate potential engagements, maintain business records, protect the website, and improve our services and content. We do not sell personal information.

Legal basis

Where applicable, we process contact information with your consent and for our legitimate interests in responding to business inquiries, operating the website, and protecting our services.

Service providers

We may use service providers for website hosting, analytics, email delivery, security, and business operations. These providers process information only to provide their services to us and are subject to their own contractual and legal obligations.

Retention

We retain inquiry information only as long as reasonably necessary to respond, manage a potential or active business relationship, meet legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To make a request, email privacy@stellarisai.com.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards. No online service can guarantee absolute security, because apparently the internet was designed before anyone considered consequences.

International use

This website may be accessed from different countries. Information may be processed in the United States or other locations where our service providers operate, subject to applicable safeguards.

Changes

We may update this policy as the website and business evolve. The effective date above indicates the latest revision.

Contact

Questions about this policy may be sent to privacy@stellarisai.com.
